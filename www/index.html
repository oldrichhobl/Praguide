Restaurant Švejk

A cavernous room in the basement of the Municipal House is home to a great big Czech food experience. Huge plates of all the good stuff come streaming from the kitchen. Go for some roasted duck, pork tenderloin, beef goulash or chicken or pork schnitzel. Washed down with lots of cold Pilsner beer, you’ll be feeling full and fine in no time. Gorgeous Art Nouveau interiors and a nice staff, reservations are recommended.
